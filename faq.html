FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Plain-language answers to the most important questions about CSCA's current status and future direction.

What is CSCA?

CSCA is a federally and provincially registered nonprofit organization in Canada focused on student support, education-oriented programming, and long-term institutional development.

Is CSCA currently a school or accredited educational institution?

No. CSCA currently operates as a nonprofit organization. This website does not claim that CSCA is an accredited school, a degree-granting institution, or a regulated provider of formal academic credit.

Why does the website look institutionally structured?

Because even new organizations benefit from clear governance language, official communications channels, serious public documentation, and visible program direction.

Does CSCA plan to work with secondary-school students?

Yes. CSCA's future direction includes education-oriented support for students in Grades 10-12, including pathway-related information and family-facing engagement, subject to applicable requirements.

Can schools or community organizations collaborate with CSCA?

Yes. CSCA welcomes early partnership conversations with student groups, families, community organizations, and education-adjacent partners whose work aligns with the organization's mission.

How can I contact CSCA?

You can email info@csca-canada.ca or visit the contact page for specific channels.
