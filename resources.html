Resources & Updates

Public updates from CSCA

This page gives the site visible activity and helps communicate that the organization is operating, building, and reachable.

April 2026

Official website launched at csca-canada.ca

CSCA launched its first formal website to establish a public institutional identity, official communications presence, and foundational organizational information.

April 2026

Foundational nonprofit structure and operations are being formalized

Following its federal and Ontario nonprofit setup, CSCA is organizing communications, records, governance language, and shared digital operations.

April 2026

Grades 10-12 support planning has begun

CSCA's future-facing planning includes education-oriented support for secondary-school students in Grades 10, 11, and 12, with emphasis on guidance, preparedness, and pathway-related information.

April 2026

Partnership inquiries are open

Student groups, community partners, and education-adjacent organizations can now reach out through official CSCA contact channels to discuss collaboration.
