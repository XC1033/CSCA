# Workspace next steps

## Best legal path right now
Because CSCA is operating as a nonprofit organization rather than an accredited school, the most realistic current path is:
1. Apply for Google for Nonprofits
2. Activate Google Workspace for Nonprofits
3. Use the domain-based inboxes on this site as the official email structure

## Why the website still helps
This site helps by showing:
- an official domain
- structured public information
- governance language
- multiple contact channels
- a live updates section
- a serious institutional tone

## When to consider a separate education-domain later
If a future school entity is created and separately qualified under the applicable legal and regulatory rules, it may make sense to launch a separate education-specific domain for that entity.
