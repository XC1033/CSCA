Privacy Policy

A simple public privacy statement for the CSCA website.

Chinese Students in Canada Alliance (CSCA) respects your privacy. This website may collect limited information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, organization name, and the contents of messages you send to CSCA by email.

Information submitted to CSCA may be used to respond to inquiries, maintain records, communicate with partners or interested individuals, and support organizational, educational, and community-facing activities.

CSCA does not sell personal information. Reasonable steps are taken to safeguard information, but no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. Questions can be sent to info@csca-canada.ca.
