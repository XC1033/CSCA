Governance

Public-facing governance and accountability structure

Because CSCA is a new organization, public trust depends on visible structure, role clarity, and responsible language.

The governance structure below reflects CSCA's founding-stage operating model. Individual names and formal appointments can be inserted as they are finalized.

Governance principles

  • Operate in accordance with the organization's legal status and stated scope.
  • Use public language that clearly distinguishes current activity from future aspirations.
  • Maintain official contact channels and records under the organizational domain.
  • Document program development and partnership engagement in a structured way.
  • Expand carefully where compliance, staffing, and policy are sufficient.
Public governance note

A simple, visible governance model helps a new organization look credible and institutionally serious. Once your names are ready, replace the role placeholders above with real people and short bios.
