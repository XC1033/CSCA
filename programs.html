Programs

Current and planned program directions

CSCA is building education-oriented programming in stages. Some areas are immediate and public-facing; others are part of the future institutional direction.

Public note: the items below describe current focus areas and development priorities. They do not represent accredited instruction, credit-granting programming, or regulated school operations unless separately stated and lawfully established in the future.

Student Support and Community Navigation

Provide practical information, referral pathways, and community-facing resources that help students and families better navigate opportunities and support systems.

Grades 10-12 Learner Support

Develop pilot content and engagement pathways for secondary-school students in Grades 10, 11, and 12, with emphasis on planning, direction-setting, and educational confidence.

Pathway and Planning Information

Explore education-oriented information sessions that help students and families understand academic pathways, expectations, and decision points relevant to the Ontario context.

OSSD-Adjacent Information Development

As part of long-term planning, CSCA may develop general information and support resources related to Ontario secondary-school pathways and OSSD-adjacent decision-making, subject to compliance review.

Leadership and Enrichment

Support workshops, small-group sessions, and community initiatives that build communication, responsibility, and leadership capacity among students.

Family and Community Engagement

Build channels for families, community groups, and aligned professionals to connect with CSCA around future educational support needs.

Program architecture

How CSCA thinks about program development

Layer What it includes Current status
Public Information Website content, basic resources, organization updates, and official communication channels. Live and expanding
Pilot Support Exploratory workshops, student engagement, family-facing information, and partnership conversations. Under development
Scaled Services Structured, repeatable education-oriented services for learner support and pathway development. Future direction
Regulated Education Activity Any future activity that would require licensing, approvals, accreditation, or formal oversight. Not current; would require separate compliance steps
